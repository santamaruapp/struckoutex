Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024

1. Introduction

StruckOutEX (the "App") is an application that records scores for "strikeout," a game where you kick a soccer ball to knock down panels. We respect our users' privacy and are committed to protecting personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Recorded in the App

  • Player names
  • Game settings (number of players, panel count, ball count, panel mode, etc.)
  • Game scores and results
  • App usage statistics (stored locally only)

2.2 Advertising-Related Information

This app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect the following information:

  • Device information (device ID, OS information, language settings, etc.)
  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Ad display counts and click history

This information is used for ad delivery and effectiveness measurement. For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personally identifiable information (address, phone number, email address, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Contact information
  • Photos or videos
  • Third-party service integration information (except advertising services)

3. Data Storage and Management

All game data recorded in this app (player names, scores, settings, etc.) is stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to external servers.

Data related to ad delivery may be sent to Google AdMob's servers, but this is only used for ad delivery and effectiveness measurement.

3.1 Data Backup

This app does not provide automatic backup to cloud services such as iCloud. Game data is managed independently on each device.

3.2 Data Synchronization

This app does not provide data synchronization across multiple devices. Data is managed independently on each device.

4. Information Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share users' personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When required by law
  • When users have given explicit consent
  • When providing information to Google AdMob for ad delivery (limited to information that cannot identify individuals, such as advertising identifiers)
  • Anonymized statistical data for app improvement (only information that cannot identify individuals)

5. Data Deletion

When the app is deleted, all data stored on the device will be deleted. You can also manually delete data from the app settings.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If children under 13 use the app, please use it under parental supervision.

7. Advertising

This app displays ads using Google AdMob in exchange for providing the app for free. Ads are displayed on the game screen but do not interfere with gameplay.

For detailed information about ads and how to change ad settings, please refer to Google AdMob Help.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated as necessary. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the app.

9. Contact Us
